“Huntin’ Ducks” is a target game. Your goal is to shoot as many ducks and “strange encounters” as possible.
If your hunt ends, click QUIT THIS HUNT, and then START HUNTIN’ to play again.
Each time to start again, please select DYNAMITE as your item. 

Controls (keyboard/mouse): 
Aim = Mouse
Shoot = Left click
Pause = Escape (Esc)
